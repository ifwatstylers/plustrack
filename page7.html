Presentation to Federation of Malaysian Freight Forwarders (FMFF) 

PLUSTrack was invited by Federation of Malaysian Freight Forwarders (FMFF) to present to their council members during their 118th FMFF Council Meeting. The meeting was held on 8 January 2022 in Penang represented by 60 council members from regional associations including Selangor Freight Forwarders & Logistics Association, Johor Freight Forwarders Association, Penang Freight Forwarders Association, Sarawak Forwarding Agencies Association, Sabah Freight Forwarders & Logistics Association and Labuan Freight Forwarders Association. This engagement platform is to increase the PLUSTrack brand presence and generate interest especially amongst the logistics companies.
